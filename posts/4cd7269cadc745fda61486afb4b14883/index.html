bitnami 版 gitlab 修改端口

转自：http://blog.csdn.net/zxgis/article/details/44096901

感谢原作者！！



一般安装包路径在： /opt/gitlab-version/

应该修改一下路径的文件
/opt/gitlab/apache2/conf/httpd.conf
Listen 80
/opt/gitlab/apache2/conf/bitnami/bitnami.conf
virtual hostdefault:80
/opt/gitlab/apps/gitlab/gitlab-shell/config.yml
/opt/gitlab/apps/gitlab/htdocs/config/gitlab.yml
